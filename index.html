WHAT IS METAMASK ?

Metamask is a crypto wallet that stores your Ethereum, which you need to purchase and mint a Kitty Crypto Gang NFT.

WHAT IS AN NFT ?

NFT stands for "Non-Fungible Token". It's a unique, digital item with blockchain-managed ownership that users can buy, own, and trade.
